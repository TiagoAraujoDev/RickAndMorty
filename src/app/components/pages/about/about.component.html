Welcome to our Rick and Morty Application!

We are thrilled to offer you a platform that allows you to access all the information about your favorite show, Rick and Morty, in one place. Our application is built using Angular, a powerful framework that enables us to create seamless and responsive user interfaces.

The application utilizes the Rick and Morty API , which provides a plethora of information about the show's characters, episodes, and locations. You can search for your favorite characters, see their information and its status, and discover new episodes and locations.
